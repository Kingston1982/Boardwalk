About The Boardwalk

Woolacombe Beach has just been awarded the title of – “2nd best beach in the UK” This is what you will look out across when you dine at The Boardwalk. Add to this — wonderfully fresh local produce, beautifully cooked and served by friendly, knowledgable staff and you have the recipe for a great meal or a great occasion. We serve a hearty breakfast, special lunchtime deals, full evening menu – or maybe just drinks on the tearrace on a fine sunny evening! Join us on our Facebook page to keep up with our latest offerings and offers, and news. We hope to see you at The Boardwalk very soon.
